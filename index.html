Notations and definitions

  • Definition Point

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} p :& \mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & (x,y) & \mapsto \mathbf{e}_{o} + x \mathbf{e}_{1} + y \mathbf{e}_{2} + \frac{1}{2} (x^2 + y^2) \mathbf{e}_{\infty} \end{array} \right.$

Notations and definitions

  • Definition Discretization cell

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} D :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \left\{ \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \right\} \\ & p_i(u,v) & \mapsto \left\{ p(x,y) \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, u-\frac{1}{2} \leq x < u + \frac{1}{2} , v-\frac{1}{2} \leq y < v + \frac{1}{2} \right\} \end{array} \right.$

  • Mostly used in the sequel

    • $ D(\mathbf{e}_0) = \left[ -\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{2} \right)^2 $

Discrete Rotation

  • Definition Rotation operator

    • $ V = \alpha = \gamma \gamma = p + q \mathbf{e}_{12}$, $p,q \in \mathbb{Z}$

  • Rotation of a point

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} r :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & p_i(x,y) & \mapsto \frac{V p_i V^{-1}}{|| V ||} \end{array} \right.$

  • Discrete rotation

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} \lceil . \rceil :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & p_i(x,y) & \mapsto \mathbf{e}_{o} + \lceil x \rceil ＋ \lceil \mathbf{e}_{1} + \lceil y \rceil\mathbf{e}_{2} + \frac{1}{2} (\lceil x \rceil^2 + \lceil y \rceil^2) \mathbf{e}_{\infty} \end{array} \right.$

Set of remainders not scaled

  • Definition

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} s :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \times \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & p_i,p & \mapsto \frac{V }{||V||} p_i - p \end{array} \right.$

  • Rotation is bijective iff.

    $ \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists! p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in D(\mathbf{e}_0)$

  • Equivalent to

    $\left\{ \begin{array}{cll} \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in D(\mathbf{e}_0) \\ \forall p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \frac{V }{||V||}D(\mathbf{e}_0) \end{array} \right.$

Set of remainders scaled

  • Definition

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} s :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \times \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & p_i,p & \mapsto V p_i - \color{red}{||V||} p \end{array} \right.$

  • Rotation is bijective iff.

    $ \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists! p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{red}{||V||} D(\mathbf{e}_0)$

  • Equivalent to

    $\left\{ \begin{array}{cll} \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{red}{||V||} D(\mathbf{e}_0) \\ \forall p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{red}{V} D(\mathbf{e}_0) \end{array} \right.$

Set of remainders scaled

  • $V = \gamma \gamma$, $|| V || = \gamma \gamma^*$

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} s :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \times \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & p_i,p & \mapsto \gamma \gamma p_i - \gamma \gamma^* p =　\gamma （\gamma p_i - \gamma^* p) \end{array} \right.$

Set of remainders reduced

  • Definition

    • $\left| \begin{array}{cll} s :& \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \times \mathbb{G}^{3,1} & \mapsto \mathbb{G}^{3,1} \\ & p_i,p & \mapsto \color{green}{\gamma} p_i - \color{green}{\gamma^*} p \end{array} \right.$

  • Rotation is bijective iff.

    $ \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists! p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{green}{\gamma^*} D(\mathbf{e}_0)$

  • Equivalent to

    $\left\{ \begin{array}{cll} \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{green}{\gamma^*} D(\mathbf{e}_0) \\ \forall p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{green}{\gamma} D(\mathbf{e}_0) \end{array} \right.$

Condition on $\gamma$

  • Constraint

    • $\left\{ \begin{array}{cll} \forall p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{green}{\gamma^*} D(\mathbf{e}_0) \\ \forall p_i \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, \exists p \in \mathbb{G}^{3,1}, s(p_i,p) \in \color{green}{\gamma} D(\mathbf{e}_0) \end{array} \right.$

  • if

    $ \gamma = (q + 1) + q \mathbf{e}_{12}$

  • then

    boundaries of $ \gamma D(\mathbf{e}_0),\gamma^* D(\mathbf{e}_0) $ between 2 L1 balls of radius $q$ and $q+1 \Rightarrow $ $s(p_i,p) \in {\gamma} D(\mathbf{e}_0)$ and $s(p_i,p) \in {\gamma^*} D(\mathbf{e}_0)$
